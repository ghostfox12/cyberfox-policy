Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

CyberFox Labs ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile applications collect, use, and protect your information.

Our Applications

Step Counter

Information We Collect

Our applications are designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Personal Profile Data: Weight, height, age, and gender — stored locally on your device only, used for calorie and distance calculations.
  • Health & Fitness Data: Step counts, daily activity history — stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and local file storage. We do not operate external servers or databases. Your data never leaves your device except when you explicitly choose to share it.

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as the application is installed. Step Counter retains up to 400 days of step history. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all associated data. You can also clear app data from your device settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

Our apps use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Google Play Billing: Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play. Payment information is handled entirely by Google. We do not collect or store any payment details.

Permissions

Our app requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality:

  • Activity Recognition: To count your steps using the device's step sensor
  • Notifications: To show your step count and daily progress
  • Internet: For displaying advertisements and processing in-app purchases

Your Rights (GDPR / KVKK)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can view all your data within the application at any time.
  • Right to Deletion: You can delete all your data by clearing app data in device settings or uninstalling the app.
  • Right to Portability: Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control and portability over your data.
  • Right to Object: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings.
  • Right to Restriction: You can disable specific permissions (activity recognition, notifications) through your device settings at any time.

Legal Basis for Processing: We process your data based on your explicit consent (granted when you use the app and accept permissions) and for the legitimate purpose of providing the app's core functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security features. Premium status is stored using encrypted secure storage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
