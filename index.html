Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

CyberFox Labs ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile applications collect, use, and protect your information.

Our Applications

Step Counter Prayer Times To Do List Water Reminder QR Code Reader Voice Recorder

Information We Collect

Our applications are designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Personal Profile Data (Step Counter): Weight, height, age, and gender — stored locally on your device only, used for calorie and distance calculations.
  • Health & Fitness Data (Step Counter, Water Reminder): Step counts, water intake — stored locally on your device only.
  • Location Data (Prayer Times): Used solely to calculate prayer times and qibla direction. Location is stored locally and not transmitted to external servers.
  • Camera Access (QR Code Reader): Used solely for scanning QR codes and barcodes. No images are stored or transmitted.
  • Microphone Access (Voice Recorder): Used solely for audio recording. Recordings are stored locally on your device.
  • Task Data (To Do List): Your tasks and notes — stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and local file storage. We do not operate external servers or databases. Your data never leaves your device except when you explicitly choose to share it.

Third-Party Services

Our apps use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Permissions

Our apps request only the permissions necessary for their core functionality:

  • Activity Recognition: Step Counter — to count your steps
  • Location: Prayer Times — to determine prayer times for your location
  • Camera: QR Code Reader — to scan QR codes
  • Microphone: Voice Recorder — to record audio
  • Notifications: Prayer Times, Water Reminder — to send reminders
  • Internet: All apps — for displaying advertisements

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all associated data. You can also clear app data from your device settings at any time.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
